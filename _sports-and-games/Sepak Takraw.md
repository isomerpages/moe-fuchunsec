<p><strong><u>Introduction<br /></u></strong>Sepak takraw is one of the more popular sports CCA in Fuchun Sec School. It is a game that is easy to pick up as you just need a takraw ball and juggle. In Fuchun Sec, you can often see students playing before school starts or during recess. It is indeed well-liked not only by the Malay students but also students of other races.</p>
