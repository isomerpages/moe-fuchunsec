<p>Parents are our significant partners in education. Fuchun Secondary School wants to work in closer collaboration with our parents to better support and help promote the holistic development of our children. Parents are&nbsp;<strong>strongly encouraged</strong>&nbsp;to sign-up for the various PSG sharing and bonding activities and events to learn, connect and interact with other parents. Please register your interest in the tinyurl link:&nbsp;<strong><a href="https://tinyurl.com/Fuchun-Secondary-School-PSG" target="">https://tinyurl.com/Fuchun-Secondary-School-PSG</a>. Alternatively, you may scan the QR code to register.&nbsp;</strong>For any enquiries, please&nbsp;contact your child&rsquo;s form teacher</p>
